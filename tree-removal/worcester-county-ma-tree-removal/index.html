As a finite being, we as humans do not have control on disasters or emergency situations, but we can control how we respond to it. Asses the Situation

From a safe distance, assess the level of damage that has been done and things that can be salvaged from the emergency scene. Doing this will enable you to offer the professionals accurate information when they arrive, which will hasten and help them in getting the situation under control.

Call on Professionals

It's not the right moment to undertake DIY; this is the time to call on professionals to come to the rescue and salvage the situation. Neglecting this will only lead to more danger.
